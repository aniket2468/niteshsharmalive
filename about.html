Our History

Myself Nitesh Sharma doing gigs and concerts since last 6 years

I started my career when I was 12 year old, I am from small village called Singiyahi which is located in Pupri, Bihar. When I was in my childhood, I used to like humming songs. One day when I was walking through with my friend, he said "your vocal is too good". I took that complement as a grant and started thinking of my career from that side. I started doing singing classes, vocals, learning instruments. With the financial economy in my family I wasn't able buy the instruments so I used to go to musical instruments shops and play there for a while. Buying those for me was dream at that age.

I came to Pune for higher studies, I did study as well as singing. I participated in Sa Re Ga Ma, I passed two stages but later I was disqualified. Later somehow I managed to buy some instruments like Guitar, Sinthesiser, etc. And I started doing gigs and concerts in hotels, restraurants, private parties, etc. Thats how I started my career and looking forward to achieve more.ðŸ˜Š
